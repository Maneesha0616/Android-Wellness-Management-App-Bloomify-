- **File**: `ic_bloomify_logo.png`
- **Sizes needed**:
  - `drawable-mdpi/`: 48x48px
  - `drawable-hdpi/`: 72x72px
  - `drawable-xhdpi/`: 96x96px
  - `drawable-xxhdpi/`: 144x144px
  - `drawable-xxxhdpi/`: 192x192px

- **Style**: Modern, friendly, wellness-themed

## Image Specifications
- **Format**: PNG (with transparency) or JPG (for backgrounds)
- **Style**: Flat design, modern, colorful
- **Colors**: Match app theme (greens, blues, pinks, teals)
- **Quality**: High resolution, crisp edges
- **Transparency**: PNG with alpha channel for icons
